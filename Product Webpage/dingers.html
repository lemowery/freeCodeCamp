Welcome to the official page for YUM Dingers!

Tested. Deadly. Dingers.

Our Dingers are hand-crafted deep within Appalachia. Laborers hurry to the rivers each morning to collect the tears of Smallmouth Bass, which are then left to ferment in batches for 10 years at a time. The fermented tears are then mixed with a concoction containing 7 secret herbs and spices, Mountain Dew, and dreams. The resulting mixture is heated to incredible temperatures, creating a thick paste which can be molded. Each Dinger is then hand-shaped into their iconic form to create this versatile fishing lure.

The Dinger is effective in many rigs, check out a couple of our favorite uses!
