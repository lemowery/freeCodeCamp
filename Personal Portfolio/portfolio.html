About Me

I come from a small town in West Virginia called Craigsville. Growing up in such a rural area fueled my love for the outdoors, so fishing, camping, and hiking are among my favorite activities, but I also developed a love for technology. I love video games and hope to someday be a part of making one. I enjoy developing software because I love to solve problem, and I am greatly satisfied when I have traced a program, develop a better understanding of it, and fix a bug. Nearing the end of my degree, I have discovered the necessity of self-learning, which has driven me to discover new technologies and begin mastering them. I hope to make software that has a positive impact in the world whether it be ensuring the product is secure, providing a product which brings entertainment, or (perhaps ambitiously) creating something that changes the world.
